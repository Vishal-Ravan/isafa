Membership Categories

1. Corporate Membership

Open for: Companies and corporations contributing to the SAF ecosystem
Annual Fee: INR 10.00 lakhs/annum (excluding taxes) USD 11,5000 per annum

Exclusive Benefits
  • 1. Opportunity to become part of the Technical committees as Chair/ Co-chair (as approved by the leadership)
  • 2. Engage with government and regulatory bodies to shape policies, incentives, and frameworks for SAF adoption
  • 3. Network with key stakeholders like airlines, fuel producers, investors, policymakers, and sustainability leaders.
  • 4. Participate in exclusive forums, working groups, and high-impact partnerships shaping SAF deployment.
  • 5. Contribute to India’s SAF roadmap while aligning with international decarbonization strategies.
  • 6. Actively participate in decision-making and strategy development for ISAFA Priority for project identification
  • 7. Nomination to various government expert committees to shape policies and initiatives
  • 8. Monthly newsletters/updates on government notifications
  • 9. Opportunity to speak at sectoral events and showcase your expertise
  • 10. Special deals and discounts for ISAFA events
  • 11. Advocacy for specific project requirements
  • 12. Brand visibility at events and other marketing collaterals of ISAFA
  • 13. Special discount offers for events by strategic partners (like CMAI, IEPRA, ASAFA, IATA, and others)
  • 14. Priority for project identification
