About us

Leading India’s charge for low-carbon air travel

▪ SAF Association is a national and global catalyst for accelerating the adoption of Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) in India.

▪ An industry-driven initiative, bringing together visionary leaders, policymakers, regulators, researchers, and investors to create a thriving SAF ecosystem.

▪ Positioning India as a global SAF hub, bridging policy, technology, and investment to drive large-scale SAF production and adoption.

▪ Transforming aviation by decarbonizing the sector and advancing Indiaʼs Net Zero 2070 vision and carbon-neutral growth.

▪ Enabling breakthrough innovation in SAF production, feedstock optimization, and supply chain efficiencies.

▪ Accelerating green investment & financing to unlock SAFʼs commercial scalability and market viability.

▪ Shaping progressive policies by working with government and regulatory bodies to create a robust SAF framework.

▪ Forging cross-border partnerships with aviation leaders, fuel producers, and global SAF Association to integrate India into the global SAF producers.

Vision

To accelerate the global transition to sustainable aviation by driving innovation, collaboration, and large-scale adoption of Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) ensuring a cleaner, more resilient, and climate-conscious future for the aviation industry.

Mission

To accelerate the global adoption of SAF by enabling innovation, investment, and policy frameworks that support scalable and commercially viable production.

To drive collaboration across stakeholders, including governments, industry leaders, technology providers, researchers, and financial institutions to build a resilient and sustainable SAF ecosystem.

Align with international climate goals and industry commitments, such as the global framework of CORSIA, IATAʼs Net Zero by 2050, and global sustainability initiatives, to ensure a unified approach to aviation decarbonization.

Enhance energy security and supply chain resilience by diversifying feedstock sources, improving production efficiencies, and advancing next-generation SAF technologies.

Create long-term economic and environmental value by fostering job creation, sustainable growth, and a greener future for the aviation industry.

SAF Association's Leadership

Mr. Jimmy Olsson

President

Mr. Jimmy Olsson, President, India SAF Association

India stands on the brink of becoming a global leader in high quality Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) production, poised to revolutionise the aviation sector with innovative, technology-driven solutions. Our vision is for India is to lead the charge in reducing Scope 3 emissions across hard-to-abate sectors, including global manufacturing, consulting, and logistics, while setting the gold standard for SAF infrastructure.

The India SAF Association is dedicated to fostering an inclusive ecosystem where technology meets purpose. By uniting stakeholders across government, industry, academia, and the farming community, we aim to catalyse SAF innovation and seamlessly integrate it into the aviation supply chain. Technology remains the cornerstone of this endeavour—whether through advanced feedstock processing, cost-efficient production methods, or cutting-edge distribution networks.

At the heart of our vision lies the aspiration to make SAF both sustainable and affordable. By leveraging India’s technological prowess and agricultural strengths, we aim to not only meet ambitious decarbonisation goals but also provide enhanced income opportunities for farmers. This holistic approach ensures that our advancements benefit not just aviation, but society at large.

Mr. Vijay Nirani

Senior Vice President

Mr. Rohit Kumar

Secretary-General

Mr. Rohit Kumar, Secretary General, CMAI

By serving as a bridge between policy, industry, and innovation, CMAI has been instrumental in bringing the momentum for the effective development of carbon markets and fostering a collective commitment to sustainability.

The India SAF Association envisions to reduce Scope 3 emissions, targeting hard-to-abate sectors such as aviation, a critical contributor to global emissions. These sectors require innovative solutions that go beyond incremental change. By integrating SAF into the aviation supply chain, the SAF Association exemplifies the potential of combining policy advocacy, industry collaboration, and forward-thinking solutions to drive impactful change.

India is leading the way in integrating sustainable fuels into its energy mix, reflecting a strong commitment to achieving Net Zero targets. Biofuels, supported by government initiatives, are reshaping energy production, reducing emissions, and empowering local economies. This synergy between sustainability and socio-economic progress is a testament to India’s visionary approach to energy transformation.

I believe that the India SAF SAF Association will stand at the forefront of a transformative journey, uniting industries to advance the adoption of SAF. This is not merely a blueprint, it embodies India’s unwavering commitment to a greener and more resilient aviation future.

Ms. Shivaaneey Rai

Strategic Lead

Mr. Vijay Nirani, Senior Vice President, India SAF Association

The aviation industry stands at the crossroads of innovation and responsibility with its growing market share and potential. Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) has emerged as a revolutionary solution to decarbonise air travel, and its adoption is not just a technological advancement but a moral imperative for achieving net-zero goals. As someone deeply engaged in the bioenergy sector, I have witnessed first hand the transformative potential of biofuels like SAF in reshaping the energy landscape.

The India SAF Association is a landmark initiative designed to address critical challenges in scaling SAF production and adoption. It creates a platform where technology, policy, and industry converge to realise the potential of SAF.

The initiative represents a significant step in India's efforts to reduce aviation emissions, with ambitious targets of a 1% SAF blend by 2027, 2% by 2028, and 5% by 2030. By leveraging its agricultural strengths, technological expertise, and policy momentum, India has the potential to become a global hub for SAF production and innovation. The initiatives outlined within this document are commitments to redefining aviation’s relationship with sustainability.

I am inspired by the collective ambition driving this initiative and confident in its ability to set new benchmarks in sustainable aviation. The path ahead is one of innovation, collaboration, and purpose—a journey that promises lasting impact on the environment and the industry.
