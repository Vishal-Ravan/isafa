About us

Leading India’s charge for low-carbon air travel

▪ ISAFA is a national and global catalyst for accelerating the adoption of Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) in India.

▪ An industry-driven initiative, bringing together visionary leaders, policymakers, regulators, researchers, and investors to create a thriving SAF ecosystem.

▪ Positioning India as a global SAF hub, bridging policy, technology, and investment to drive large-scale SAF production and adoption.

▪ Transforming aviation by decarbonizing the sector and advancing Indiaʼs Net Zero 2070 vision and carbon-neutral growth.

▪ Enabling breakthrough innovation in SAF production, feedstock optimization, and supply chain efficiencies.

▪ Accelerating green investment & financing to unlock SAFʼs commercial scalability and market viability.

▪ Shaping progressive policies by working with government and regulatory bodies to create a robust SAF framework.

▪ Forging cross-border partnerships with aviation leaders, fuel producers, and global SAF alliances to integrate India into the global SAF supply chain.

Vision

To accelerate the global transition to sustainable aviation by driving innovation, collaboration, and large-scale adoption of Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) ensuring a cleaner, more resilient, and climate-conscious future for the aviation industry.

Mission

To accelerate the global adoption of SAF by enabling innovation, investment, and policy frameworks that support scalable and commercially viable production.

To drive collaboration across stakeholders, including governments, industry leaders, technology providers, researchers, and financial institutions to build a resilient and sustainable SAF ecosystem.

Align with international climate goals and industry commitments, such as the global framework of CORSIA, IATAʼs Net Zero by 2050, and global sustainability initiatives, to ensure a unified approach to aviation decarbonization.

Enhance energy security and supply chain resilience by diversifying feedstock sources, improving production efficiencies, and advancing next-generation SAF technologies.

Create long-term economic and environmental value by fostering job creation, sustainable growth, and a greener future for the aviation industry.
