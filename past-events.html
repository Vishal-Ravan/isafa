Launch of ISAFA
Pioneering the Future of Sustainable Aviation in India

A Defining Moment for Aviation Sustainability

ISAFA was officially launched on 4th February 2025, marking a transformative step in India's aviation decarbonization journey.

A landmark industry-led initiative, uniting key stakeholders to accelerate the adoption and commercialization of Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF).

Strategically aligned with global net-zero commitments, positioning India as an active contributor to sustainable aviation initiatives.

Establishing a collaborative ecosystem, bringing together policymakers, industry leaders, and innovators to shape the future of SAF.

1st Event Post-Launch-
"Decarbonisation of Aviation Sector"

First industry-wide engagement following the launch of ISAFA.

▪ Discussions on policy, investment, and technology innovations for Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) adoption.

▪ Participation from government officials, airline executives, SAF producers, and sustainability leaders.

▪ Panel discussions & keynote addresses on India's role in aviation decarbonization.


▪ Strategic roadmap for implementing SAF blending targets and scaling production capabilities.
